Post Incorporation

Sole proprietorship, Corporation

Post incorporation is a process of incorporating a company. In this process, an individual or group of people start a new company and then register it with the government.

. The process of incorporation is not complicated but it does require some work. The first step is to register the company name with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC). Once this has been done, you can then apply for the Certificate of Incorporation which will be issued to you after your application has been accepted.
